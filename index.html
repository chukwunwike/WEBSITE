About me

Full stack Developer

I’m Chukwunwike Achi, but people call me “Ace.” I’m a frontend and backend developer, and I specialize in CSS & HTML,kotlin,python,dart,flutter,sql that just work across all platforms and browsers. I care deeply about building interfaces that are usable and pleasant for the most number of people possible.

My professional life has been 100% driven by my passion for design and structure. I started building static websites for small businesses while in school, then developed an app for my friend in my university days to share file over the internet with python. For three years, I’ve worked for an e-commerce platform in ibadan as a freelance developer, where I was able to drive a 20% growth in sales by shaving five seconds from the website’s loading time. After that, I was in charge of implementing and launching the mobile app for a remote study center in ibadan, oyo state, which I was able to complete in just under three months. Not that learning Dart and flutter at the same time made things easy for me. Still, in the end, I was able to deliver an app that’s used by hundreds of students and tutors daily and did so on-time and on-budget.

Right now, I’m excited about learning Django and how it can help speed up my workflow. In the following years, I also plan to explore the “progressive web app and ios” more and become a better-rounded full-stack dev.
